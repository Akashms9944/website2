The Hearth

One fire. No shortcuts.

Salt & Char has a single wood-fired hearth at its center, and every dish that leaves the kitchen has passed through it. We buy whole animals and whole fish, split our own oak and almond wood, and let smoke and salt do most of the seasoning. The menu is short because the fire only has so much room.
